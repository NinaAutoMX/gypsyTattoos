Who We Are

Where Spirituality Meets Ink

Creative Vision Spiritual Customisation

Gypsy Tattoos is an independent travelling Tattoo and Piercing Studio that combines creative vision, spirituality, and customisation. What began with tattooing at Markets quickly expanded into something bigger.

I bring the tattoo and piercing experience to you. I operate outside of traditional studios, offering a personalised, thoughtful approach to tattoos that honours each client's unique story and spiritual journey. I travel extensively, drawing inspiration from other cultures and spiritual traditions to develop my craft and to provide unique, culturally sensitive craftsmanship.

This independent, nomadic lifestyle enables me to provide a safe, personal environment where clients can discover and express their individuality through my services. Get in touch or pop in anytime.
