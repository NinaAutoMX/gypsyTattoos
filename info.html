Meet the Artist

Michelle Bosch

My life has always had meaning and purpose because of art and creativity. Tattoo Artistry was the obvious decision, starting with anime, movies, and media and then combining that with the love of art history. My path started in art studies in school, continued through film school, and finally brought me to the tattoo industry.

My main styles: I love bold black and grey illustrative styles mixed with abstract shapes and forms. However, I am also deeply committed to continuously improving my skills and expanding my expertise across a wide range of tattoo styles.
